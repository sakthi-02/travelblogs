About Me

This is what I do.

Travel smart, travel light: Budget travel hacks exist! Flexible dates and shoulder seasons can ease the financial squeeze. Consider hostels, guesthouses, or cooking your own meals to save. Pack versatile layers that mix and match, roll clothes to save space, and only bring essentials.

Safety first, then explore! Stay alert, make copies of important documents, and inform someone of your itinerary. Travel insurance provides peace of mind. Embrace the local culture - learn a few phrases, visit bustling markets, and strike up conversations.

Get inspired, get going! Dive into travel blogs like The Blonde Abroad or Nomadic Matt for tips and destination insights. National Geographic Traveler and Lonely Planet guidebooks offer in-depth knowledge. Follow inspiring travel accounts on social media, or get armchair travel vibes with documentaries like "Eat Pray Love" or movies like "Lost in Translation."
